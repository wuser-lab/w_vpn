# W VPN Privacy Policy — publisher draft

**Effective date:** replace before publication

W VPN provides encrypted network connectivity. We do not record browsing
activity, DNS queries, traffic content, destination IP addresses, or connection
history tied to an account. We do not sell personal data or use VPN traffic for
advertising.

We process the minimum information needed to operate the service: an account
identifier, subscription entitlement, registered device public keys, selected
gateway region, assigned tunnel address while active, and security or service
events needed to prevent abuse. Payment details are processed by Apple, Google,
or the published web payment provider; W VPN receives transaction identifiers
and entitlement status rather than full card details.

Aggregate gateway health measurements may include load, latency, packet loss,
and error counts without stable account identifiers. Diagnostics leave the
device only after explicit user action and must be reviewed before sending.

Replace this draft with publisher legal details, lawful bases, retention
periods, subprocessors, international-transfer terms, data-subject rights,
contact information, and the competent supervisory authority before release.
